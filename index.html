Our Story

A Journey From the Heart of Morocco

Dar Zitoun — "House of the Olive Tree" — was born from a deep love for Moroccan culinary heritage. Our founder, Chef Fatima El Alaoui, brought her grandmother's recipes from the ancient city of Fès, where the art of slow cooking and bold spices has been perfected over centuries.

Every tagine, every couscous, every pastilla is prepared with the same care and intention as if cooked for family. We source our spices directly from the souks of Marrakech and Fès, ensuring every dish carries the authentic fragrance of Morocco.
